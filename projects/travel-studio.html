CASE STUDY

Project Overview

The Travel Agency Platform is a modern web application designed for travel companies to present destinations, manage tours and simplify online booking.

Visitors can browse available tours, filter destinations, explore detailed travel information and submit booking requests through an intuitive interface.

The platform combines attractive presentation with practical business functionality, helping travel agencies improve customer experience and streamline tour management.
